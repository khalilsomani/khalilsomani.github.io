About

My name is Khalil Somani and I am a junior at the University of Washington in Seattle, double majoring in Human Centered Design & Engineering (B.S.) and Psychology (B.S.). I am passionate about human-centered research, user-friendly design and design thinking, technical communication, and data collection/analytics.

Washington Trails Association Rebranding, HCDE 308

About the Client: Washington Trails Association

As the nation's largest state-based hiking nonprofit organization, Washington Trails Association (WTA) is the voice for hikers in Washington state. They protect hiking trails and wildlands, take volunteers out to maintain trails, and promote hiking as a healthy, fun way to explore the outdoors.

Service: UX/Graphic Design

Using Adobe Illustrator, I rebranded the WTA website as well as mobile landing pages and subpages. I also created a new logo, buisness card, and visual design system. This system consisted of brand identity, brand attributes, brand colors, graphics, client overview, and a typographic system.

Traveler's Almanac,
HCDE 310

About the Client: Prospective Travelers

Along with partner Allison Chiu, I created an application for travelers to create a live feed of flight information to allow their customers to plan a trip by considering factors like the weather at their destination, the price of their flight, and pictures of their intended vacation spot.

Service: UX/Web Development

We accomplished this with Google App Engine using Python, HTML, and CSS. We incorperated three different API's in order to complete this project: Rome2rio, Wunderground, and Flickr. Take a look at our demonstration below!
